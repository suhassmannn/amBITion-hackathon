What is NGO Hub?
NGO Hub is a portal that connects various NGOs with volunteers and donors, facilitating better coordination and support for social causes.
How can I use NGO Hub?
You can create an account, log in, and browse through various NGOs and their ongoing projects. You can also submit requests for specific needs or volunteer to support ongoing initiatives.
Is NGO Hub free to use?
Yes, NGO Hub is completely free for both NGOs and volunteers. Our aim is to support social initiatives without financial barriers.
How do I contact support?
You can reach out to our support team via email at support@ngohub.com or through our contact form on the website.
